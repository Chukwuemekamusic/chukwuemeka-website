# Chukwuemeka Music Artist Website - Specification

## Overview
A modern, simple, and artistic website for Christian musician Chukwuemeka - a psalmist who illuminates hearts and minds with God's love through reflective and diverse musical storytelling.

## Brand Identity
- **Artist**: Chukwuemeka (Nigerian artist based in Aberdeen)
- **Genre**: Soulful Gospel with classical, jazz, and worship influences
- **Mission**: Illuminate hearts and minds with the love of God
- **Unique Position**: Psalmist who reflects on life in its fullness - beauty and struggle - through God's lens, ending in reassurance
- **Audience**: Both churched and unchurched listeners seeking calm and peace

## Design Philosophy

### Visual Aesthetic
- **Modern & Simple**: Clean, minimalist design emphasizing content and visual storytelling
- **Artistic Feel**: High-quality imagery, thoughtful typography, generous white space
- **Peaceful Atmosphere**: Reflecting the calm and peace his music brings
- **Cross-Cultural Appeal**: Subtle design that welcomes both Christian and secular audiences

### Color Palette Suggestions
- **Primary**: Deep blues and warm grays (conveying peace, trust, depth)
- **Secondary**: Soft golds and cream tones (representing divine light, warmth)
- **Accent**: Gentle earth tones (grounding, authenticity)
- **Alternative**: Monochromatic with selective color highlights

## Technical Specifications

### Core Requirements
- **Responsive Design**: Mobile-first approach
- **Performance**: Fast loading times, optimized images

### Music Integration
- **Streaming Embeds**: Spotify, Apple Music widgets
